Christmas concert

This is a tradition since the very beginning of the HD street band. As a matter of fact, the band's very first concert was a Christmas concert in this very same venue, next door to our rehearsal room.

The setlist will, of course, include some of the most popular Christmas music, but be ready for some surprises as well.

At the end of the concert, we will share a Christmas cake with everyone present. Will you be there?
