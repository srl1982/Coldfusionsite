Summer sounds Festival

Our band is proud to be one of the selected concert band to play the Redville 's Summer Sounds festival.

For our last concert in Redville, we'll have the honour to play in the city's center for performing art at 7:00pm, as support act for the Glenn Miller Orchestra that will play right after us at 8:30 pm.

This is the concert not to be missed by anyone supporting us, so, come and join us for this exceptional event.
