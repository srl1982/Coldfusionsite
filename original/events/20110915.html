Back to school concert

Once again HD street bad will play a back to school concert at the Baton Rouge high's auditorium.

As you probably know, our band includes lots of teachers and faculty staff members from various schools around the city and the parish, so we'll be more than happy to honour our beloved teachers one more time.

This year, HD Street band will add some pop-music arrangements to its classic big band music set list.
